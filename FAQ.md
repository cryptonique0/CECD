# FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is CECD?
CECD (Community Emergency Coordination Dashboard) is a blockchain-powered platform for coordinating emergency response efforts.

### Is it free to use?
Yes, the platform is free. However, blockchain transactions require gas fees in CELO.

### Which blockchains are supported?
Currently, we support Celo Mainnet and Alfajores Testnet.

## Wallet & Blockchain

### Do I need a wallet?
Yes, you need a Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.) to access blockchain features.

### How do I get CELO tokens?
You can purchase CELO on exchanges like Coinbase or use the Celo faucet for testnet tokens.

### Are transactions reversible?
No, blockchain transactions are permanent and cannot be reversed.

### What are gas fees?
Gas fees are small amounts of CELO required to process blockchain transactions.

## Donations

### Where do donations go?
Donations go directly to the emergency fund managed by the multi-signature wallet.

### Can I donate with stable coins?
Yes, you can donate using CELO, cUSD, or cEUR.

### Can I track my donations?
Yes, all transactions are recorded on the blockchain and can be viewed on block explorers.

## Privacy & Security

### Is my data private?
Blockchain transactions are public, but personal data is encrypted and stored securely.

### Who can see my wallet address?
Your wallet address is visible when you make transactions or interact with the platform.

### Is the platform secure?
We follow best practices for blockchain security, but users are responsible for securing their private keys.

## Technical

### Does it work offline?
Yes, the platform has offline support with a service worker that caches data locally.

### Which browsers are supported?
Modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

### Is there a mobile app?
The platform is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that can be installed on mobile devices.
